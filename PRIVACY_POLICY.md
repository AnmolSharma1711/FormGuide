# Privacy Policy - Form Field Explainer

**Last Updated: January 2, 2026**

## Data Collection and Usage

Form Field Explainer collects and processes the following information to provide AI-powered form guidance:

### Information Collected:
- Form field labels and placeholder text
- Field types (email, text, etc.)
- Surrounding context text (up to 500 characters)
- Page domain name
- Selected language preference

### How We Use Your Data:
- Data is sent to our secure backend server
- Processed by Azure OpenAI to generate helpful guidance
- Used only to provide real-time assistance
- **Not stored** permanently on our servers
- **Not shared** with third parties (except Azure OpenAI for processing)

### Third-Party Services:
- **Azure OpenAI**: Processes form context to generate guidance
- Subject to Microsoft's privacy policy

### Data Security:
- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- No personally identifiable information is permanently stored
- No user accounts or tracking

### Your Rights:
- You can disable the extension at any time
- Data processing stops immediately when disabled
- No data is retained after processing
